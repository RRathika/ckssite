Connected Devices Testing

The world of connected devices testing is expanding rapidly. This micro-credential includes the testing of mobile phones, smartphones, tablets, mobile apps, and the Internet of Things (IoT). If your company works with devices that connect to a network and the Internet, you are your team needs the specialized knowledge of Connected Devices Testing.

CyberSecurity Testing

Every testing team needs to understand acceptance testing and this micro-credential helps do that. The goal of acceptance testing is for the targeted user or operator to “accept” the software as working to meet their requirements for the software. Prove your knowledge of how different types of users can conduct acceptance testing in different environments.

DevOps Testing

DevOps bridges the gap between development and operations by bringing the two teams together for the entire software lifecycle, from development to delivery. DevOps Testing (also called DevTestOps and QAOps) is a critical, ongoing part of DevOps and the focus of this micro-credential.
